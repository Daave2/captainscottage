Welcome to Captain's Cottage

Nestled in the heart of Scarborough, North Yorkshire, Captain's Cottage is a charming mid-terrace house built in the 1800s. This traditional fisherman's cottage now offers a modern seaside escape with tasteful nautical accents.

Enjoy authentic maritime charm paired with contemporary comforts.

Accommodation Details

The cottage accommodates up to four guests across two beautifully appointed bedrooms:

  • Double Bedroom: First floor for ample space and comfort.
  • Twin Bedroom: Second floor with roof windows and enchanting night sky views.

The bathroom is fully equipped with a bath, a shower over the bath, WC, and basin.

Living Spaces & Modern Amenities

Enjoy a well-appointed kitchen/diner complete with modern appliances:

  • Electric oven and induction hob
  • Microwave
  • Pod coffee machine
  • Fridge and freezer
  • Washing machine
  • Dishwasher

Relax in a cozy sitting room featuring an electric fire, Smart TV with Freeview, WiFi, and a curated selection of books and games.

Outdoor & Additional Amenities

Step outside to enjoy a spacious decking area, ideal for al fresco dining or a quiet moment by the sea.

  • Pet Policy: One well-behaved dog is welcome.
  • Family-Friendly: Highchair and travel cot available on request.
  • Parking: A roadside parking permit is provided.

Local Attractions

Located in Scarborough's historic district, you're within walking distance of local restaurants, shops, and the beachfront. Explore nearby attractions such as:

  • Scarborough Castle: Historic site with panoramic views. (English Heritage)
  • Scarborough Harbour: Seafront cafés and yacht club ambiance.
  • North Bay Beach: A sandy haven ideal for families.
  • Peasholm Park: Themed gardens and a boating lake. (Council)
  • Maritime Heritage: Discover the town’s seafaring past. (Heritage Centre)
  • Art Gallery: Local art and culture. (Gallery)
  • Visit England: More attractions. (VisitEngland)
