Your Seaside Retreat

Captain's Cottage is a lovingly refurbished 19th-century mid-terrace home nestled in Scarborough's historic Old Town. With its rich maritime heritage and contemporary comforts, it offers the perfect base for exploring the Yorkshire coast.

Recently renovated in 2024, the cottage combines period character with modern amenities. From the cosy living room with its electric fireplace and Smart TV to the fully equipped kitchen and peaceful garden retreat, every detail has been considered for your comfort.

Accommodation:

  • First floor: Elegant double bedroom with period features
  • Second floor: Light-filled twin bedroom with roof windows
  • Modern bathroom with bath and separate shower
