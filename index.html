Explore Scarborough & Beyond

Discover Nearby Attractions

Captains Cottage is perfectly situated to explore the best of Scarborough on foot:

  • Scarborough Castle (Approx. 300m): Historic fortress with stunning coastal views.
  • South Bay Beach & Harbour (Approx. 500m): Sandy beach, amusement arcades, boat trips, and fresh seafood stalls.
  • Scarborough Spa (Approx. 1km): Victorian venue hosting concerts and events.
  • Rotunda Museum (Approx. 800m): Fascinating geology museum.
  • Peasholm Park (Approx. 1.5km): Beautiful oriental-themed park with boat hire and miniature naval battles in summer.
  • North Bay Railway (Approx. 1.5km): Miniature railway leading to the Sea Life Sanctuary.
  • Stephen Joseph Theatre (Approx. 1km): Renowned theatre founded by Alan Ayckbourn.
  • Anne Bronte's Grave (Approx. 700m): Located in St Mary's Churchyard.
