Captain's Cottage

A Historic Seaside Retreat in Scarborough

Welcome to Captain's Cottage

Nestled in the heart of Scarborough, North Yorkshire, Captain's Cottage is a charming mid-terrace house built in the 1800s. This traditional fisherman's cottage offers a blend of historic character and modern amenities, creating an idyllic seaside retreat for families and couples.

Experience the authentic taste of Scarborough’s maritime heritage combined with contemporary comforts.

Accommodation Details

The cottage accommodates up to four guests across two beautifully appointed bedrooms:

  • Double Bedroom: Located on the first floor with ample space and comfort.
  • Twin Bedroom: Situated on the second floor, featuring roof windows with enchanting night sky views.

The bathroom is fully equipped with a bath, a shower over the bath, WC, and basin.

Living Spaces & Modern Amenities

Enjoy a well-appointed kitchen/diner complete with modern appliances:

  • Electric oven and induction hob
  • Microwave
  • Pod coffee machine
  • Fridge and freezer
  • Washing machine
  • Dishwasher

Relax in a cozy sitting room featuring an electric fire, Smart TV with Freeview, WiFi, and a selection of books and games.

Outdoor & Additional Amenities

Step outside to enjoy a spacious decking area, perfect for outdoor dining or relaxing after a day at the beach.

  • Pet Policy: One well-behaved dog is welcome.
  • Family-Friendly: Highchair and travel cot available on request.
  • Parking: A roadside parking permit is provided for convenience.

Explore the Local Attractions

Located in Scarborough's old town, you’re within walking distance of restaurants, shops, and the beachfront. Notable nearby attractions include:

  • Scarborough Castle: A historic site with panoramic coastal views.
  • Scarborough Harbour: Enjoy seafront cafés and charming yacht club vibes.
  • North Bay Beach: A beautiful, sandy haven perfect for families.
  • Peasholm Park: Japanese-style landscaped gardens offering picturesque scenery.
