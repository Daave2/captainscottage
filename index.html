Captain's Cottage

A Historic Seaside Retreat in Scarborough

Welcome to Captain's Cottage

Nestled in the heart of Scarborough, North Yorkshire, Captain's Cottage is a charming mid-terrace house built in the 1800s. This traditional fisherman's cottage blends historic character with modern amenities, creating an idyllic seaside retreat for families and couples.

Enjoy the authentic taste of Scarborough’s maritime heritage combined with the comforts of contemporary living.

Accommodation Details

The cottage accommodates up to four guests across two beautifully appointed bedrooms:

  • Double Bedroom: Located on the first floor with ample space and comfort.
  • Twin Bedroom: Situated on the second floor, featuring roof windows offering enchanting views of the night sky.

The bathroom is fully equipped with a bath, a shower over the bath, WC, and basin.

Living Spaces & Modern Amenities

The cottage features a well-appointed kitchen/diner complete with modern appliances, including:

  • Electric oven and induction hob
  • Microwave
  • Pod coffee machine
  • Fridge and freezer
  • Washing machine
  • Dishwasher

The cozy sitting room offers an electric fire, Smart TV with Freeview, WiFi, and a variety of reading materials and games for your entertainment.

Outdoor & Additional Amenities

Step outside to enjoy a spacious decking area, perfect for outdoor dining or unwinding after a day at the beach.

  • Pet Policy: One well-behaved dog is welcome.
  • Family-Friendly: Highchair and travel cot available upon request.
  • Parking: A roadside parking permit is provided for your convenience.

Explore the Local Attractions

Located in Scarborough's old town, you're within walking distance of local restaurants, shops, and the beachfront. Must-see attractions include:

  • Scarborough Castle: Historic site with panoramic coastal views.
  • Scarborough Harbour: Enjoy seafront cafés and charming yacht club vibes.
  • North Bay Beach: A beautiful, sandy haven perfect for families.
  • Peasholm Park: Japanese-style landscaped gardens offering picturesque scenery.
