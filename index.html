Captains Cottage – Charming Seaside Retreat

Captains Cottage is a newly refurbished 19th-century cottage in Scarborough’s historic Old Town, offering an ideal blend of maritime charm and modern comfort. Located at 35 Longwestgate, Scarborough, YO11 1QB, this mid-terrace home accommodates up to 4 guests in two lovely bedrooms and provides the entire home for your exclusive use.

Guests love the authentic character – this former fisherman’s cottage is around 170 years old – combined with contemporary amenities introduced by the passionate new owners in mid-2024. Step out of the front door and you’re just minutes from Scarborough’s golden beaches, harbor, and castle, yet tucked away in a peaceful residential street for a restful stay.

Sleeps 4 Comfortably

The cottage features two tastefully decorated bedrooms spread over the upper floors, providing comfortable sleeping arrangements for up to four guests:

  • Master Bedroom (First Floor): A tranquil retreat featuring a spacious double bed with quality linens, ample storage, and charming coastal touches. Full of character, this elegant room comfortably sleeps two.
  • Twin Bedroom (Second Floor): Bright and inviting, this room offers two full-size single beds under a skylight that provides glimpses of the coastal sky. It’s a perfect space for children or additional guests, continuing the cottage’s seaside theme.

Modern Bathroom

A modern family bathroom is located on the first floor, featuring a full-size bathtub with an overhead shower, plus sink and toilet. Fresh towels, complimentary toiletries, and a heated towel rail are provided for a touch of spa-like comfort.

Living, Kitchen & Amenities

Cozy Living Area

Relax in the ground-floor living room, furnished with plush sofas and an electric log-effect fireplace for a warm ambience. Enjoy entertainment on the large flat-screen Smart TV (Freeview & apps) with a DVD player, or connect with free high-speed WiFi throughout the cottage. A selection of books, family board games, and puzzles is also available for quiet evenings or rainy days.

Fully Equipped Kitchen & Dining

The well-appointed kitchen-diner has everything you need for self-catering. It’s fitted with an electric oven, efficient induction hob, microwave, dishwasher, and a full-size fridge/freezer. You’ll find all essential cookware, utensils, toaster, electric kettle, and even a pod coffee machine. A wooden dining table comfortably seats four.

A washing machine (washer/dryer function mentioned in reviews) is also provided, along with essential supplies like washing-up liquid, dishwasher tablets, and tea towels, so you can pack light.

Private Outdoor Deck & Parking

A rare find in Scarborough's Old Town, Captains Cottage boasts a wonderful private outdoor deck and small garden area. Accessed via the kitchen's back door, the raised decking features a table, chairs, and sun umbrella – perfect for al fresco dining or enjoying the sea breeze. The area is enclosed for privacy.

Enjoy rooftop views while you relax or dine outdoors.

Convenient Parking

Forget parking worries! We provide a complimentary on-street parking permit (no reservation needed), allowing you to park free of charge in the local residents' zone. Spaces are usually available nearby. Once parked, many attractions are within easy walking distance.

(Note: Like many Old Town properties, the cottage opens directly onto the street. Please supervise children and pets when entering/exiting.)

Pet Friendly

We happily welcome one well-behaved dog FREE of charge (please inform us when booking). The enclosed deck is convenient, and Scarborough offers dog-friendly beaches (seasonal rules apply) and walks nearby. Dog bowls and waste bags are provided.
